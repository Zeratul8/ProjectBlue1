"For versions 2021 and below, the newtonsoft-json package must be installed."

"Go to 'Window' -> 'Package Manager', then click the '+' button in the top left.
Select 'Add package by name', enter 'com.unity.nuget.newtonsoft-json', and click 'Add'."
